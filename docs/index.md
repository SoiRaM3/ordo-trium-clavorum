The Codex is a living body of texts, maintained with restraint and transparency. Documents are listed in their intended reading order.

## Supplementary Materials

Additional materials supporting the Codex may be found elsewhere in the repository, including:

---

## Principle of Order

The Codex is arranged to move from identity to discipline, from structure to limitation. Readers are encouraged to approach it sequentially for clarity.

The authority of the Codex lies not in assertion, but in coherence.
