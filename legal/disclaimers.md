## No Legal or Sovereign Authority

The Order does **not** claim and expressly disclaims:

* sovereignty or state recognition
* diplomatic or governmental status
* judicial or coercive authority
* immunity or privilege under civil law

## No Ecclesial Authority

The Order is not a church, denomination, or ministry. It claims no sacramental authority, apostolic succession, or teaching office.

Participation in the Order does not replace membership in a church or obedience to lawful ecclesial authority.

---

## No Political or Military Role

The Order has no political mandate and engages in no political advocacy, campaigning, or partisan activity.

It maintains no armed force, militia, or military function. All knightly language and symbolism are devotional and disciplinary only.

---

## Voluntary Participation

Participation in the Order is entirely voluntary. Individuals may withdraw at any time without penalty or consequence beyond loss of membership.

No document of the Order imposes obligation on non-members.
