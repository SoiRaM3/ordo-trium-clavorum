# Order of the Three Nails

**Ordo Equitum Trium Clavorum**
*Motto: Per Clavos Firmamur — By the Nails We Are Made Firm*

---

## What This Repository Is

This repository contains the **official public documents** of the **Order of the Three Nails**, a modern Christian neo‑chivalric lay order founded in **2025**.

It exists to provide clarity, transparency, and accountability regarding the Order’s identity, structure, symbols, and limits. All core texts are maintained as version‑controlled documents so that the Order’s claims and practices remain stable, reviewable, and resistant to myth‑making.

---

## What the Order Is

The Order of the Three Nails is:

* Christian in faith and devotion
* Knightly in form, not monastic
* Lay in structure, not ecclesial
* Modern in origin, without medieval continuity
* Symbolic and disciplinary, not political or military

Its spiritual center is the **Three Nails of the Passion of Christ**, understood as signs of obedience, constancy, and faithful endurance.

---

## What the Order Is Not

The Order explicitly does **not** claim to be:

* A sovereign or state‑recognized order
* A church, denomination, or ministry
* A bearer of sacramental or apostolic authority
* A continuation or revival of a medieval order
* A political, militant, or territorial body

Membership confers no legal title, privilege, or authority outside the internal life of the Order.
